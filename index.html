# Privacy Policy for SlateLog US Effective date: June 12, 2026 SlateLog US is an offline production logging app for recording local shoot-day notes, shot logs, gear checks, media handoff details, and optional local reminders. ## Data Collection SlateLog US does not collect personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, third-party SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, remote databases, or external upload services. ## Local Storage Projects, clients, shoot dates, manually entered locations, director notes, cinematographer notes, editor notes, shot logs, gear checklist items, media card details, delivery formats, and reminders are stored locally on the user's device. ## Network Use SlateLog US does not connect to the internet and does not upload video footage, project records, gear lists, handoff notes, or reminders. ## Notifications Local reminders are off by default. If the user chooses to enable a reminder, iOS may ask for notification permission. Reminder scheduling remains local to the device. ## No Professional Advice SlateLog US is an independent recordkeeping tool. It does not provide legal, union, insurance, permit, licensing, or copyright advice. ## Business Model SlateLog US is sold as a one-time paid download. It does not include subscriptions, in-app purchases, trials, paywalls, or paid unlock states. ## Contact For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
